The role of a Tech Lead is not easy to understand, for several reasons. The TL role is a hybrid one, not quite a manager, and not quite an individual contributor.

If I tell you how I became a tech lead you’re going to laugh. You see, I became a tech lead first in my head, and later in reality. There I was, a fresh Eng 2 who just came back from a leadership training for which my manager at the time had nominated me because he saw leadership potential in me. This training left me so inspired and fired up that for the first time, I felt a sense of alignment and clarity about where I wanted my career to head towards. I remember telling my manager in our 1:1 after coming back: “I want to be a tech lead and I want to help you build this team”.
